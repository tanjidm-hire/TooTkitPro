Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF to Excel converter free to use?

Yes, our basic PDF to Excel conversion is completely free with no registration required. We offer premium features for advanced users who need additional functionality.

How accurate is the table detection?

Our advanced algorithms achieve over 95% accuracy in detecting and preserving tables from PDF documents. Complex tables with merged cells or unusual formatting may require minor adjustments after conversion.

Is my data secure when using this tool?

Absolutely. All file transfers are encrypted, and your documents are automatically deleted from our servers after conversion. We never store or share your files.

What is the maximum file size I can convert?

The free version supports PDF files up to 50MB. For larger files or batch processing, consider our premium plans.

Can I convert scanned PDFs to Excel?

Scanned PDFs require OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology. Our premium version includes OCR capabilities to convert scanned documents to editable Excel files.
