Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free to use?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free with no hidden charges or watermarks.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No, all processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your computer.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can convert between these formats during compression.

What's the maximum image size I can compress?

There's no strict limit, but very large images (above 20MP) may take longer to process.
