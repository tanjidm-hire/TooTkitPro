How to Use the Passport Photo Cropper

  1. Select the target country from the dropdown list. The required dimensions (in millimeters) will be shown.
  2. Click "Upload Image" and choose a high-quality photo from your device.
  3. An adjustable crop box will appear over your image, maintaining the correct aspect ratio for the selected country.
  4. Move and resize the crop box to frame your head and shoulders according to typical passport photo guidelines (check specific country rules).
  5. Click the "Crop & Preview" button.
  6. Review the preview. The final image will be generated at a standard resolution (e.g., 300 DPI) matching the required physical size.
  7. Click "Download Cropped Photo" (JPG or PNG) to save the image to your device.

Why Use This Tool?

Getting passport or visa photos correctly sized can be tricky. This tool simplifies the process by providing standard dimensions for many countries and an easy-to-use cropping interface. It ensures your digital image meets the size requirements before you print it.

Disclaimer: While this tool sets the correct dimensions and aspect ratio, always double-check the *latest* official passport photo requirements for your specific country regarding background, head size percentage, expression, and forbidden items (glasses, headwear unless for religious reasons).
